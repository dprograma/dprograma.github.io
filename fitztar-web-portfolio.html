Overview

The Fitztar website is the conversion-focused front door to the Fitztar ecosystem — introducing the product, communicating its value, and funnelling visitors into the mobile app. Built with Next.js and TypeScript for fast, SEO-friendly rendering.

It presents the app’s core features, pricing and community story with a responsive, animation-rich interface, and connects directly to the app’s onboarding and download flows.

Key Features

  • Server-rendered Next.js for SEO & speed
  • Responsive, animation-rich landing experience
  • Feature, pricing & community sections
  • App store funnel & onboarding hooks
  • Reusable component design system
  • Optimised Core Web Vitals
