Overview

Fitztar is a fitness and wellness app built with React Native for iOS and Android, backed by a cloud-based Node.js microservices architecture. As CTO of Inpruva, I owned the technical roadmap and led a team of 4 engineers through agile 2-week sprints from first concept to a live production launch.

The product combines real-time activity tracking, personalised workout and nutrition planning, and a gamified rewards engine — leaderboards, achievement badges and streak tracking — with a secure in-app wallet for accumulating and redeeming reward points. A social community engine drives retention through progress sharing, challenges and smart habit reminders.

Key Features

  • Real-time activity & biometric tracking
  • Personalised workout & nutrition plans
  • Gamified rewards: badges, streaks, leaderboards
  • Secure in-app wallet for reward points
  • Wearable API integration for biometric sync
  • Social community engine & challenges
  • Smart push notifications (SNS/SQS)
  • Real-time analytics dashboard, 99.5% availability
