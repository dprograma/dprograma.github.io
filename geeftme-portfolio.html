Overview

Geeftme is a crowdfunding platform that lets individuals and organisations raise funds for causes, projects and personal needs. As CTO of Silicon Harbour Transprojects, I architected and led the build of the entire system — a fault-tolerant microservices backend engineered for reliability, compliance and scale.

The platform processes NGN donations through Paystack and international payments through CrayFi, with automated transaction splits, settlement reconciliation and fundraiser wallet management. A Celery Beat scheduler drives automated financial operations including pending-donation recovery, withdrawal processing and exchange-rate updates.

Key Features

  • Webhook-based payment processing with idempotent handling
  • Automated settlement reconciliation & transaction splits
  • Fundraiser wallets with withdrawal workflows
  • Multi-currency: NGN via Paystack, international via CrayFi
  • Real-time fraud detection & validation pipelines
  • KYC/AML integration for regulatory compliance
  • Redis caching & connection pooling (−25% query latency)
  • Dockerised CI/CD with zero-downtime rolling deploys
