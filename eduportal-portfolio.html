Overview

Eduportal is an educational platform that closes the gap between educational goals and technological possibilities — providing seamless, intuitive tools that enhance the learning experience. It centres on educational materials, online examinations and a comprehensive e-learning environment.

Built with PHP/Laravel and MySQL, it hosts publications and e-books, delivers online exams, and creates a connected environment for learners and educators.

Key Features

  • Educational materials, publications & e-books
  • Online examinations engine
  • Comprehensive e-learning environment
  • Learner & educator accounts
  • Secure authentication
  • Content management
